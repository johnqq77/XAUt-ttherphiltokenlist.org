XAUT (Tether Gold) Whitepaper

Introduction

Tether Gold (XAUT) is a blockchain-based digital asset issued by Tether, backed by physical gold. Each XAUT token represents ownership of one troy ounce of fine gold on a specific gold bar, held in secure Swiss vaults.

Vision

To provide a trusted, convenient, and decentralized way to access and transfer gold ownership digitally across global markets.

Key Features

  • 1:1 Gold-Backed
  • Secure Storage in Swiss vaults
  • Blockchain-based: Ethereum and TRON
  • Redeemable & Transferable
  • Audited & Compliant

Tokenomics

  • Token Name: Tether Gold
  • Ticker: XAUT
  • Standard: ERC-20 / TRC-20
  • Backing: 1 Troy Ounce of Physical Gold
  • Supply: Based on demand and reserves

Use Cases

  • Digital Trading
  • Wealth Preservation
  • Portfolio Diversification
  • Global Transfers

Security & Transparency

Gold reserves are regularly audited. Token holders can verify gold bar serials. KYC/AML applies for redemptions.
